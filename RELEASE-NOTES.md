## APP-FINANCAS-PESSOAIS v1.1.0 ☁️

**Released:** March 9, 2026  
**Try it now:** [https://financas-pessoais-two.vercel.app](https://financas-pessoais-two.vercel.app)

### 🎯 What's New

We're thrilled to announce the **first complete release** of your personal finance companion! This version brings everything you need to take control of your money, beautifully designed and available on any device.

### ✨ Highlights

**📊 Visual Dashboard**  
Get an instant overview of your financial health with colorful cards showing your net worth, income, expenses, and savings at a glance.

**📈 Interactive Charts**  
Visualize your money with beautiful, interactive charts:
- Track how your wealth grows over time
- See where your money goes by category
- Compare income vs expenses month by month

**🌙 Dark Mode**  
Easy on the eyes, day or night. Switch themes with a single tap and your preference is saved automatically.

**📱 Install as an App**  
Add to your home screen and use it like a native app - works offline, loads instantly, and feels just like a regular app on your phone.

**📄 Export Reports**  
Generate professional PDF reports of your finances with one click. Perfect for sharing with your accountant or keeping records.

**💼 Complete Finance Management**  
- Track income and expenses
- Manage multiple bank accounts
- Monitor loans and installments
- Keep tabs on investments
- Set up recurring payments
- Organize with custom categories

### 🎨 What It Looks Like

The app features a modern, clean design with:
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Intuitive navigation
- Mobile-first responsive layout
- Professional color-coded cards
- Clear, large numbers for easy reading

### 📱 How to Get Started

**On Mobile:**
1. Visit [financas-pessoais-two.vercel.app](https://financas-pessoais-two.vercel.app)
2. Tap your browser menu
3. Select "Add to Home Screen"
4. Enjoy your new finance app!

**On Desktop:**
Just visit the URL and start managing your finances immediately.

### 💡 Pro Tips

- **Dark Mode:** Look for the sun/moon icon in the top right
- **Quick Add:** Use the + buttons to quickly add transactions
- **PDF Reports:** Find the export option in settings
- **Offline:** Once installed, works without internet

### 🔒 Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your financial information. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed.

### 🆓 Completely Free

No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden costs. This is a passion project made to help people take control of their finances.

### 🙏 Thank You

To everyone who tested early versions and provided feedback - this wouldn't be possible without you!
